## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

TriDash is an actively developed realtime on-chain system focused on synchronized market interaction and deterministic round-based settlement.

The project explores how blockchain applications can operate under strict timing constraints while maintaining transparent and verifiable outcomes.

Unlike traditional asynchronous DeFi interaction, TriDash experiments with coordinated execution windows shared across participants.
