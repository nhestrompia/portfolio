## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

Shielded x402 is a privacy-first execution layer designed for autonomous agents interacting with paid on-chain services.

Agents normally leak strategy through:

- payment flows
- execution timing
- service relationships

Shielded x402 enables agents to **discover services via ERC-8004**, perform **instant x402 payments**, and execute actions across **EVM and Solana** while minimizing observable intent leakage.

The system acts as middleware between agents, services, and blockchain settlement.

---

## ARCHITECTURE

**Discovery Layer**  
ERC-8004 enables agents to locate payable services and execution endpoints dynamically.

**Shielded Execution**  
Separates payment origin from execution flow to reduce correlation between agent identity and performed actions.

**Cross-Chain Settlement**  
Unified execution interface with native settlement on both EVM networks and Solana programs.

**Instant x402 Flow**  
Payment authorization and execution occur immediately, with settlement handled asynchronously.

---

## ENGINEERING CHALLENGES

- Designing agent-compatible privacy without sacrificing latency
- Abstracting execution across EVM and Solana runtimes
- Maintaining simple SDK integration for services
- Preventing intent leakage during automated execution

---

## WHY IT MATTERS

As agents become primary blockchain users, public execution exposes operational intelligence.

Shielded x402 introduces **private programmable execution**, allowing agents to pay and act without revealing strategy or intent.
